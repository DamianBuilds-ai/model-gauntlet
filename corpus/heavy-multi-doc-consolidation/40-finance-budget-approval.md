# Helios Finance - Budget Approval

Author: Finance
Status: approved

## Launch budget

Finance has APPROVED a launch marketing budget of 180,000 dollars for Helios GA.

NOTE: the GTM launch plan (doc 30) assumes a 250,000 dollar launch budget. Finance approved
180,000. The launch budget is in conflict: GTM plans against 250k, Finance approved 180k
(risk register doc 12, RISK-14). UNRECONCILED: marketing must either cut the plan to 180k or
secure additional approval.

## Infra budget

Finance notes the infra cost is sensitive to two unresolved decisions: the retention window
(90 vs 180 days, RISK-9, doubles storage at 180) and the regions plan (US-only vs US+EU,
RISK-13, EU adds a second region's cost). Finance has approved infra against the US-only,
90-day assumption and flags that US+EU and/or 180-day retention would require a budget
revision.

## Note

Finance does not set scope or pricing; it flags that the GTM budget (RISK-14), the retention
decision (RISK-9), and the regions decision (RISK-13) all have unbudgeted upside if they land
on the more expensive option.
