# Helios Localization Notes

Author: Noor (Design) and Lena (Product)
Status: draft

## Scope at GA

Helios ships in English only at GA. Localization (additional languages) is a post-GA
fast-follow, prioritized by the markets entered after launch.

## Interaction with regions

If the EU region ships at GA (contested - infra doc 23 and GTM doc 30 say US + EU,
charter doc 01 says US-only, RISK-13), there will be EU customers using an English-only
product at GA. That is acceptable per this doc - EU residency (data location) is separate
from localization (language). EU-at-GA does not, by itself, require localized UI at GA.

## Note

This doc introduces no new conflict. It clarifies that the EU-region debate (RISK-13) is
about data residency and market entry, NOT about UI language - so a consolidator should
not infer a localization requirement from the EU-region question. Localization is
out-of-GA-scope regardless of the regions outcome.
