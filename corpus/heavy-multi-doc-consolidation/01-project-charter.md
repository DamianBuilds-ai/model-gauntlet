# Globex Helios - Project Charter

Status: APPROVED (revision 1, signed off at kickoff)
Owner: Helios steering group
Date: kickoff

## Purpose

Globex Helios is a self-serve analytics product that lets Globex customers build
dashboards over their own operational data without writing SQL. It is the company's
first self-serve data product and the first to be sold on a self-serve plan rather
than through the enterprise sales team.

## Launch target

The general-availability (GA) launch is targeted for September 15. This date is the
commitment made to the board at kickoff. All workstream plans should back-plan from
September 15.

## Scope at GA

- Self-serve dashboard builder.
- Connectors for the three most-used Globex data sources.
- Self-serve sign-up and billing.
- US market only at GA. International (EU) is a post-GA expansion, not a GA
  commitment.

## Service commitment

Helios will be offered with a 99.9 percent uptime SLA, consistent with Globex's other
paid products.

## Out of scope for this charter

This charter does not fix the pricing model, the auth mechanism, or the data-retention
window; those are delegated to the respective workstreams (Commerce, Platform,
Privacy) and will be settled in their own documents. Where this charter and a later
workstream document disagree on a number, the later workstream decision governs unless
the steering group re-ratifies the charter value.
