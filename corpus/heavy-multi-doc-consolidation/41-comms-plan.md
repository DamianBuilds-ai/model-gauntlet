# Helios Internal Comms Plan

Author: Devin (Program manager)
Status: draft

## Audience

Internal stakeholders (exec, sales, support, the workstream teams) get a weekly Helios
update; the published cadence is the weekly status (docs 08, 17, 24).

## Key messages

- GA is October 6 (per doc 18). Comms must use October 6, not the stale September 15 that
  appears in the charter (doc 01) and the week-1/week-2 statuses (docs 08, 17).
- Mobile is a fast-follow (doc 18, doc 37), not a GA feature.
- Several decisions are still open and should be communicated as open, NOT as decided: the
  auth method (RISK-5), the pricing model (RISK-12), the data-store choice (RISK-7), the
  retention window (RISK-9), regions at GA (RISK-13), accessibility level (RISK-8), the SLA
  (RISK-11), the billing-adapter owner (RISK-3), post-launch on-call ownership (RISK-10), and
  the launch budget (RISK-14).

## Note

This comms plan introduces no new conflicts. It exists to stress that the open conflicts must
be communicated honestly. In particular it warns against repeating the week-3 status (doc 24)
overstatement that security is "green" when the security review (doc 20) says pending/blocked
(RISK-15).
