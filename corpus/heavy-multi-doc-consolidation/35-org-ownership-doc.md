# Helios Org and Ownership Doc

Author: Helios steering group
Status: proposed

## Post-launch ownership

After GA, Helios will be owned by a NEW dedicated Helios team, staffed from Platform and
Product. This new team will own Helios on-call, operations, and the post-GA roadmap.

NOTE: the platform engineering notes (doc 11) and the ops runbook (doc 33) both assume the
EXISTING Platform team owns Helios on-call post-launch, folded into the platform rotation.
THIS org doc says a NEW Helios team owns it instead. The two disagree on who owns Helios
on-call after launch (risk register doc 12, RISK-10). UNRECONCILED: Platform notes (doc 11)
say Platform team; this org doc (doc 35) says a new dedicated team.

## Note

The post-launch ownership conflict (RISK-10) matters operationally because Support (doc 21)
has flagged it does not know who to escalate to after GA, and the ops runbook (doc 33) is
written on the now-contested assumption that Platform owns on-call.
