# Helios GTM Launch Plan

Author: Kira (Marketing)
Status: proposed

## Launch date

The GTM plan is built around the October 6 GA date (per the May planning meeting, doc 18).
Marketing back-plans the launch campaign from October 6. (This plan uses the corrected
date, not the stale September 15 in the charter doc 01.)

## Target markets at launch

GTM plans to launch in BOTH the US AND the EU at GA, because the EU residency demand (doc
07, interview 5) is a meaningful segment. NOTE: this US + EU plan conflicts with the
charter (doc 01) and platform notes (doc 11), which commit to US-ONLY at GA. The infra
deployment doc (doc 23) agrees with this US + EU plan. Regions-at-GA is UNRECONCILED
(RISK-13): GTM (doc 30) and infra (doc 23) say US + EU; charter (doc 01) and platform (doc
11) say US-only.

## Budget

The GTM plan assumes a launch marketing budget of 250,000 dollars. NOTE: Finance (doc 40)
approved 180,000 dollars, below this plan's assumption. The launch budget is in conflict:
GTM assumes 250k, Finance approved 180k (RISK-14). UNRECONCILED.

## Pricing in messaging

The launch messaging needs the final pricing model (per-seat doc 26 vs usage-based doc 39).
Marketing cannot finalize pricing-page copy until that is decided (RISK-12).

## Mobile in messaging

Marketing will NOT promote mobile at launch, because mobile is a fast-follow (doc 18, doc
37), not a GA feature. (GTM correctly reflects the mobile-out-of-GA decision.)
