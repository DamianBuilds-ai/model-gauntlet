# Helios Mid-Project Retro Notes

Author: Devin (Program manager)
Status: notes

A mid-project retro held around the May planning meeting (doc 18). Introduces no new
conflicts; it reflects on the existing ones.

## What went well

- The dashboard builder (doc 02) and connectors (doc 03) hit their dev milestones.
- The beta gave strong signal (research doc 07), even with 4 churn (doc 31).

## What hurt

- Too many decisions were deferred at kickoff (doc 13) and never closed: auth (RISK-5),
  pricing model (RISK-12), retention (RISK-9), data store (RISK-7). These open conflicts are
  now the schedule risk that forced the GA move to October 6 (doc 18).
- The charter (doc 01) fixed a date (September 15) without securing the dependencies, so the
  date had to move (RISK-2).
- Documents drifted: the Free-tier row cap (RISK-4), the SLA (RISK-11), the regions plan
  (RISK-13), and the billing owner (RISK-3) ended up stated differently in different docs
  because no single source was made authoritative.

## Action item

Establish a single authoritative decision log so conflicts like RISK-3 through RISK-15 are
closed in one place. (This action item is itself not yet done - the conflicts remain open
across the docs as of this corpus.)
