# Helios Legal and Compliance Notes

Author: Dana-Lee (Legal)
Status: requirements

## Accessibility

Helios MUST conform to WCAG 2.1 level AA at GA. This is a procurement requirement for
public-sector buyers and several enterprise customers (see research doc 07, interview 9)
who will not purchase a product below AA. Level A is insufficient for these buyers.

NOTE: the design notes (doc 10) and the design auth notes (doc 15) plan WCAG 2.1 level A at
GA, with AA as a fast-follow. THIS legal doc requires AA AT GA. The two disagree on the
accessibility level at GA (risk register doc 12, RISK-8). UNRECONCILED: Legal requires AA at
GA; Design plans A at GA. This is material because shipping at level A could lose
procurement-gated deals.

## Data processing and residency

Legal notes that if the EU region ships at GA (contested, RISK-13), EU data-protection
obligations apply, which raises the importance of the retention decision (90 vs 180 days,
RISK-9) and the deletion flow (doc 28).

## Contractual SLA

Legal observes that whatever SLA is committed (99.9 charter doc 01 vs 99.5 ops doc 33,
RISK-11) becomes a contractual term and must match what sales puts in contracts (doc 31).
Legal does not set the SLA but flags that the two internal numbers must be reconciled before
any contract is signed.
