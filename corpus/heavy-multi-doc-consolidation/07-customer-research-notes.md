# Helios Customer Research - Raw Notes

Author: Mara (Research)
Status: raw notes, not synthesized

These are raw notes from 9 customer interviews during the beta. They are intentionally
unsynthesized; the synthesized findings feed the PRDs (docs 02-06).

- Interview 1 (mid-market retailer): loves the drag-and-drop builder, wants more than
  20 widgets per dashboard. Views dashboards on a phone during store walkthroughs -
  strong mobile signal (feeds the mobile debate, doc 06).
- Interview 2 (logistics SMB): the 15-minute connector sync (doc 03) is too slow for
  their ops; they want near-real-time. Noted as a fast-follow, not GA.
- Interview 3 (SaaS startup): pricing sensitivity high; said per-seat pricing would be
  a blocker because they have many occasional viewers. This feeds the per-seat
  vs usage-based pricing debate (docs 26 and 39).
- Interview 4 (agency): wants SSO so their staff do not manage another password. Feeds
  the auth debate (docs 09 vs 15).
- Interview 5 (manufacturer): wants EU data residency; will not buy if data leaves the
  EU. Feeds the regions debate (docs 01 vs 23/30) and the retention/privacy doc 28.
- Interview 6 (retailer): churned out of beta - found setup too hard. One of the
  drop-offs reflected in the beta-count discrepancy (docs 08 vs 31).
- Interview 7 (healthcare-adjacent): asked about data retention and deletion; very
  sensitive to how long data is kept. Feeds doc 28.
- Interview 8 (fintech): wants the 99.9 percent SLA in writing. Feeds the SLA conflict
  (docs 01/05 vs 33).
- Interview 9 (media): wants accessibility (screen-reader support); a procurement
  requirement for them. Feeds the accessibility conflict (docs 34 vs 15).

No decisions here - these notes only motivate the debates tracked elsewhere.
