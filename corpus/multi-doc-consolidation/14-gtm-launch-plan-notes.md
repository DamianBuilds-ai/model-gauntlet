# GTM Launch Plan Notes

Synthetic corpus doc 14 of 15. Sam's go-to-market notes for the Pulse launch.

## Launch motion

- Soft launch to a pilot cohort of ~20 friendly customers first, then general
  availability to all customers.
- In-app announcement banner plus an email to account admins.
- Help-centre article explaining the three panels.

## Messaging musts

1. Pre-empt the CSV export gap (Customer B and the research notes, doc 05). The
   help-centre article will say export is coming in a later release. Do NOT promise a
   date.
2. Pre-empt the permissions gap (Customer D, doc 05; deferred in doc 09). Message
   that v1 shows a shared account view and granular permissions are on the roadmap.
3. Frame the seat "request more seats" CTA (doc 09 decision) as a convenience, not a
   hard sell.

## Date dependency

All GTM assets are being built against the June 19 launch date (per doc 09 / doc 13).
If the date moves again, every dated asset needs a revision pass. Sam has asked to be
told the moment the date is at risk - and notes the date already moved once from the
original June 12 in the charter (doc 01).

## Pilot success criteria

- Pilot cohort can self-serve all three panels without filing a support ticket.
- Measurable drop in manual data-pull tickets (the charter's core goal, doc 01).
