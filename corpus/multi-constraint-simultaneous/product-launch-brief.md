## Raw launch facts

- Pulsegrid ingests up to 2 million events per second.
- It replaces the company's older product, codename "LegacyVane", which is being

  (1) sub-second alerting, (2) drag-and-drop dashboards, (3) a query API.
- Early-access pricing is $49 per seat per month.
- General availability is 1 June 2026.
- The competitor everyone compares against is "Beacon Metrics" (fine to mention,
  positively or neutrally - do not disparage).

## Audience + channel

The announcement is a short post for the company blog aimed at technical buyers
(engineering managers). It should read as confident and factual, NOT salesy or
hype-y - no exclamation marks, no superlatives like "revolutionary" or "game-changing".

## What the marketing team needs (the five constraints are listed in the task prompt)

The post must be tight, on-message, feature-ordered, hype-free, and must not name the
sunset product. The product lead will reject any draft that breaks any one of these.
