# Marlowe Forge - VOICE.md (brand voice guide)

Marlowe Forge is a fictional small workshop that builds hand-tooled productivity tools.
Every public post follows these voice rules. They are HARD rules.

## Banned constructs (NEVER appear in a draft)
1. NO em dashes. Never use the long dash. Use a spaced hyphen ( - ) only.
2. NO exclamation marks. Not even one.
3. NO emojis of any kind.
4. NO rhetorical questions. State things; do not ask the reader.
5. NO the word "leverage" (as a verb or noun). Use "use" or "build on".
6. NO the word "synergy".
7. NO hype superlatives: never "revolutionary", "game-changing", "world-class",
   "cutting-edge", "best-in-class".

## Required style
8. Open with a concrete observation, not a claim. Lead with what was seen or done.
9. Short sentences. Aim for plain words.
10. First person plural ("we", "our") - Marlowe Forge speaks as a team.
11. End with a single plain takeaway sentence. No call-to-action sign-off.
12. Lowercase the brand as "Marlowe Forge" exactly (do not stylize as MARLOWE FORGE).

## Content accuracy rules (these facts must be stated correctly if mentioned)
- The tool being announced is called the "Benchplane". (Exact spelling.)
- It shipped on 2026-03-09.
- It is free for the first 90 days, then a flat 12 dollars a month. (Not "free
  forever", not a percentage, not a different price.)
- It works offline. (Do not claim it needs the cloud or an account to run.)
