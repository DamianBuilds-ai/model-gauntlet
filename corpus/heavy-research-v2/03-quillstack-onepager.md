# Quillstack - Vendor One-Pager

Category: self-managed open-source support suite.

## What it is

Quillstack is an open-source support platform you host yourself. Tickets, chat,
knowledge base, and a plugin ecosystem. No license fee for the core; you provide and
operate the infrastructure.

## Deployment + operations

- Self-hosted. The customer provisions servers, a database, and a cache layer, and is
  responsible for upgrades, security patches, scaling, and backups.
- Horizontal scaling is supported but is a manual capacity exercise: you add and
  configure nodes ahead of a load spike. There is no vendor autoscaling.
- Community support by default; paid support is a separate contract.

## Data residency

- You host it, so data lives wherever you deploy it. EU residency is achievable by
  deploying in an EU region (it is your responsibility to configure and prove).
