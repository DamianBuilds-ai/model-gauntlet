# Helmsdesk - Vendor One-Pager

Category: managed all-in-one helpdesk (SaaS).

## What it is

Helmsdesk is a hosted support platform. Tickets, live chat, a knowledge base, and
basic reporting in one product. You sign up, configure, and run it; the vendor operates
the infrastructure, patching, scaling, and backups.

## Deployment + operations

- Fully managed. No servers for the customer to run.
- Autoscaling is handled by the vendor; the customer does nothing during a load spike.
- Setup is configuration only (no infrastructure build). Typical onboarding 2 to 3
  weeks for a small team.

## Data residency

- EU data residency is available on the Business tier and above. Customer contact data
  can be pinned to an EU region. (See the compliance memo, doc 09, for how this maps to
  Cardinal's legal line.)

## Pricing (list)

- Business tier: per-agent monthly subscription. See the three-year cost analysis
  (doc 07) for the all-in number including operating time.

## Notes

- AI assist is an add-on, not bundled, and is out of scope for this decision.
- Knowledge base and live chat are included at the Business tier.
