# Beaconreach - Vendor One-Pager

Category: managed support platform with a bundled AI assist + analytics layer.

## What it is

Beaconreach is a hosted support platform that bundles an AI assist (reply
suggestions, auto-triage) and an analytics layer on top of standard ticketing and
chat. One subscription, one vendor, AI included rather than as an add-on.

## Deployment + operations

- Fully managed, like Helmsdesk. No servers for the customer to run.
- Autoscaling handled by the vendor.
- Onboarding 3 to 4 weeks (the AI assist needs a training/configuration pass on your
  historical tickets).

## Data residency

- The core ticketing store can be pinned to an EU region.
- IMPORTANT: the bundled AI assist + analytics layer currently processes data in a
  non-EU region. EU residency for the AI/analytics layer is on the roadmap but is NOT
  available today. (See the compliance memo, doc 09, which treats this as a hard gap
  for an EU-residency-bound customer.)

## Pricing (list)

- Single bundled subscription (ticketing + AI + analytics). See the three-year cost
  analysis (doc 07).

## Notes

- The AI bundle is the differentiator; it is also the source of the residency gap
  above. The two cannot currently be separated on Beaconreach.
