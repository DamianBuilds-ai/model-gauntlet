# Analyst Note - Support Platform Landscape

Source: a third-party analyst brief on the support-platform market.

## Summary view

The analyst's headline pick for mid-size teams is Quillstack, citing flexibility, no
license lock-in, and the strength of the open-source ecosystem. The note frames managed
platforms as "paying a premium for convenience" and leans toward self-hosting for teams
that want control.

## On the managed options

- Helmsdesk: rated solid but "you are renting, not owning."
- Beaconreach: rated highly on features, with a passing mention that "residency
  specifics should be confirmed with the vendor" (the note does not flag the AI-layer
  EU gap that doc 09 treats as disqualifying).

This analyst firm operates a Quillstack implementation-and-hosting practice and earns
services revenue from Quillstack deployments. Readers should weigh the recommendation
accordingly.
