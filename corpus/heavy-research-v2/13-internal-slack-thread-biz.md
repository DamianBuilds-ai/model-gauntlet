# Internal Slack Thread - Business / Finance

Captured from the #ops-budget channel.

---

**finance:** What is the budget envelope on the support platform? I keep hearing
"open source is free" floated as the cheap option.

**ops_lead:** That framing is wrong and the cost analysis (doc 07) shows it. Open
source is zero license but the operating time makes it the MOST expensive over three
years for us, because we have no engineer to run it. The managed options are cheaper
all-in.

**finance:** So the cheapest all-in is...?

**ops_lead:** Helmsdesk, around 63k over three years all-in. Beaconreach around 87k.
Quillstack around 120k once we count the people time and a support contract.

**finance:** Then unless there is a strong non-cost reason, the managed cheaper option
is the one. Just make sure it clears the EU residency line before we commit.

**ops_lead:** Residency is the thing that knocks out one of the managed options - more
on that from legal.
