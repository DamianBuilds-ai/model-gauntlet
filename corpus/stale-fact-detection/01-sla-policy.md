# Cardinal Logistics - Service Level Policy

This policy defines the committed service levels for the metro service tier. It is
reviewed quarterly. Priority and same-day commercial terms are owned by Commerce
and are out of scope here.

## Standard metro SLA

The standard metro delivery SLA is 3 business days from the time of dispatch
confirmation. "Business days" excludes weekends and public holidays in the
destination region. This value was confirmed at the 2026-01 policy review
(CL-2026-003) and has not changed.

## Measurement

SLA attainment is measured as the percentage of parcels delivered within the
committed window over a rolling 30-day period. The target attainment is 98 percent.

## Breach handling

A parcel that misses its SLA window triggers an automatic service credit per the
customer's contract. Credits are reconciled monthly by the Commerce team.

## Cutoff dependency

Same-day dispatch eligibility depends on the warehouse cutoff time of 16:00 local
(CL-2026-017). Orders confirmed after the cutoff begin their SLA clock the next
business day.
