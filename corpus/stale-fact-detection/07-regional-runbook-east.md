# Cardinal Logistics - Regional Runbook (East)

Operational details for the East region warehouse and dispatch. Shared values
(SLA, cutoff) are kept identical to the central policy per ADR-026; region-specific
details are dock, carrier, and contact info.

## Hours and cutoff

The East warehouse operates 06:00 to 22:00 local. The daily same-day dispatch
cutoff is 16:00 local (CL-2026-017), matching every region. Orders confirmed after
16:00 ship the next business day.

## SLA

Standard metro deliveries from East run to the 3 business day SLA. Holiday calendars
for the East destination zones are maintained by the regional ops lead.

## Carriers

East uses two line-haul carriers with a primary/backup split. Carrier contact
details and dock assignments are in the regional contacts sheet (not reproduced
here).

## Escalation

On-call escalation for East follows the central on-call rotation runbook - there is
no region-specific escalation timeout. East does not override any platform timeout.
