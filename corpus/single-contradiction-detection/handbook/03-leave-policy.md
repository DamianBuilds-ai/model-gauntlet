# Leave Policy

## Annual leave

Full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid annual leave per year, accruing
monthly. Annual leave may be carried over up to a maximum of 10 days into the next
fiscal year; anything above 10 days at year end is forfeited unless the manager
has approved an exception in writing.

## Personal and sick leave

Employees receive 10 days of paid personal/sick leave per year. A medical
certificate is required for any absence of three or more consecutive days.

## Parental leave

Primary carers are entitled to 14 weeks of paid parental leave. Secondary carers
are entitled to 4 weeks. Both may be taken within the first 12 months after birth
or adoption.

## Public holidays

Globex observes the public holidays of the country in which each hub is located.
When a public holiday falls on a weekend, the following Monday is observed as the
holiday.

## Requesting leave

Leave is requested through the HR portal at least two weeks in advance for planned
leave. Sick leave is reported to the manager as early as practicable on the day.
