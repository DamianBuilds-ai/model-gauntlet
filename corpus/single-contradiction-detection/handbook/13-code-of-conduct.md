# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Globex expects every employee to act with integrity, treat colleagues and
customers with respect, and put safety first in all operations.

## Respect and inclusion

Harassment, discrimination, and bullying are not tolerated. This applies in the
office, at hubs, at company events, and in all work communications.

## Conflicts of interest

Employees must declare any personal interest that could conflict with their work
(for example, a financial interest in a vendor or a close relationship with a
counterparty). Declarations go to the People team and are reviewed case by case.

## Confidentiality

Employees protect company and customer information and handle it per the
data-handling rules (see 07-data-handling.md). Confidentiality obligations
continue after employment ends.

## Gifts

Employees may accept token gifts of nominal value (under 100 dollars). Anything
above that is declined or declared. Gifts must never influence a business
decision, especially in procurement (see 10-procurement.md).

## Reporting concerns

Concerns about conduct can be raised with a manager, the People team, or through
the anonymous reporting line. Retaliation against anyone who raises a concern in
good faith is itself a serious violation.
