# Expenses and Travel

## General principle

Spend the company's money as if it were your own. Claims must be reasonable,
business-related, and supported by a receipt.

## Approval thresholds

- Expenses up to 250 dollars: no pre-approval required, claim with receipt.
- Expenses from 250 to 2,000 dollars: require manager pre-approval.
- Expenses above 2,000 dollars: require manager and director pre-approval.

These thresholds are per item, not per claim. Procurement of goods and services
above 2,000 dollars also follows the procurement policy (see 10-procurement.md).

## Travel

Economy class is standard for all flights under six hours. Flights of six hours or
more may be booked premium economy with director approval. Hotels are booked
through the corporate travel tool; the nightly cap is 220 dollars in standard
cities and 320 dollars in designated high-cost cities.

## Per diem

A meal per diem of 60 dollars per day applies when travelling overnight for
business. Receipts are not required for the per diem itself.

## Submitting claims

Claims are submitted through the expense tool within 30 days of the expense being
incurred. Claims submitted before the 20th of the month are reimbursed in that
month's pay cycle (see 02-employment-basics.md).
