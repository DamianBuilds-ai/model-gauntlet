# Customer SLA

## Availability commitment

Globex commits to 99.9 percent monthly availability for the customer portal and
the freight-tracking API. Planned maintenance windows are excluded from the
availability calculation and are announced at least 72 hours in advance.

## Support response targets

- Priority 1 (service down): first response within 1 hour, 24/7.
- Priority 2 (major feature impaired): first response within 4 business hours.
- Priority 3 (minor issue / question): first response within 1 business day.

Business hours for support are 08:00 to 18:00 in the customer's regional hub time
zone, Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays.

## Service credits

If monthly availability falls below 99.9 percent, affected customers receive a
service credit on a published sliding scale, applied to the next invoice. Credits
are requested within 30 days of the affected month.

## Data and reporting

Customers can request an availability report for any month. Incident details that
touch a customer are shared per the incident-response communication rules (see
08-incident-response.md).
