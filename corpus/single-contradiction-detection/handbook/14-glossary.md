# Glossary

Common terms and standard values referenced across this handbook.

- **Audit log**: a structured record of authentication events, privileged actions,
  and data access, emitted by every production system to the central log platform.
  Standard retention is 90 days (see 07-data-handling.md and 08-incident-response.md).

- **Core hours**: the 10:00 to 15:00 local window during which staff are expected
  to be reachable (see 02-employment-basics.md).

- **Hub**: one of the three regional sites (North, Central, South) where Globex
  operates (see 01-company-overview.md).

- **Least privilege**: granting the minimum access needed for a role, reviewed
  quarterly (see 06-security-policy.md).

- **Restricted data**: the most sensitive data tier - customer personal and
  payment data (see 07-data-handling.md).

- **SEV1 / SEV2 / SEV3**: incident severity levels (see 08-incident-response.md).

- **SLA**: the service-level commitment Globex makes to customers - 99.9 percent
  monthly availability (see 09-customer-sla.md).

- **Session token**: the credential issued at sign-in; expires after 30 days of
  inactivity (see 06-security-policy.md).

- **SSO**: single sign-on through the company identity provider.
