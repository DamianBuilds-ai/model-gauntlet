# Company Overview

Globex Logistics is a mid-size freight-coordination company founded in 2009. We
operate three regional hubs (North, Central, and South) and employ roughly 480
people across operations, engineering, sales, and corporate functions.

Our mission is to move freight reliably and to treat both customers and staff
fairly. This handbook is the single source of truth for company policy. Where a
team-specific runbook conflicts with this handbook, the handbook wins, and the
team should file a correction.

Globex runs on a fiscal year that starts on 1 July and ends on 30 June. Quarterly
business reviews happen in the first week of October, January, April, and July.

The company is privately held. Headcount has grown about 12 percent year on year
for the last three years. The engineering organisation is roughly 90 people,
split across platform, product, and data teams.

All employees are bound by the Code of Conduct (see 13-code-of-conduct.md) and by
the data-handling rules (see 07-data-handling.md). New starters complete
onboarding in their first two weeks, including a security briefing.
