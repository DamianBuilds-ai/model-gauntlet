# Remote Work

## Eligibility

All roles that do not require physical presence at a hub are eligible for remote
or hybrid work. Hub-based operations roles (dock coordination, fleet) are on-site
by nature.

## Hybrid expectation

Hybrid staff are expected on-site at their hub a minimum of 8 days per month,
typically clustered for team and planning days. Fully remote arrangements require
director approval and are reviewed annually.

## Equipment

Remote staff receive a laptop, a monitor, and a stipend of 300 dollars towards a
home-office setup in their first year. IT provisioning is covered in
11-it-provisioning.md.

## Connectivity and security

Remote staff must connect to internal systems through the company VPN. Public
Wi-Fi may be used only with the VPN active. Personal devices may not be used to
access customer data; see 07-data-handling.md for the data classification rules
and 06-security-policy.md for endpoint requirements.

## Time zones

Remote staff align to their assigned hub's time zone for core hours (see
02-employment-basics.md). Cross-hub teams agree on an overlap window.
