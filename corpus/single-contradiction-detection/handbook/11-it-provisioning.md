# IT Provisioning

## New starter setup

IT provisions a new starter's accounts and equipment before their start date,
triggered by the People team's onboarding ticket. Standard issue is a laptop, an
identity-provider account, email, and access to the role's baseline tool set.

## Standard equipment

- Laptop (refreshed every 3 years).
- One external monitor (two on request for engineering and design roles).
- Headset.

Remote staff also receive the home-office stipend described in 05-remote-work.md.

## Access requests

Access beyond the baseline is requested through the access portal and approved by
the resource owner, consistent with least-privilege (see 06-security-policy.md).
Access is reviewed every quarter.

## Software

Standard software is pre-installed and centrally managed. Additional software is
requested through IT; any new subscription also follows procurement
(see 10-procurement.md).

## Offboarding

On an employee's last day, IT disables all accounts and collects equipment.
Account disabling is completed the same day; data owned by the departing employee
is reassigned to their manager.
