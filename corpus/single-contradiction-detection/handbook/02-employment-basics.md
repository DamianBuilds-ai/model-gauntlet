# Employment Basics

## Working hours

Standard full-time hours are 38 hours per week. Core collaboration hours, during
which staff are expected to be reachable, are 10:00 to 15:00 in their local hub
time zone. Outside core hours, staff manage their own schedules in agreement with
their manager.

## Probation

New employees serve a probation period of three months. During probation, either
party may end the employment with one week of notice. After probation, the notice
period is four weeks for both the employee and the company.

## Pay cycle

Salaries are paid monthly, on the last business day of each month. Expense
reimbursements (see 04-expenses-and-travel.md) are paid on the same cycle when the
claim is approved before the 20th of the month.

## Performance reviews

Formal performance reviews happen twice a year, in March and September. Promotion
decisions are made in the September cycle and take effect from the start of the
next fiscal quarter.

## Job levels

Engineering uses six job levels, L1 through L6. L4 is the "senior" threshold. The
level framework and the expectations at each level are maintained by the People
team and reviewed annually.
