# Benefits

## Health

Globex provides private health cover for all permanent employees, with the option
to add dependants at the employee's cost through payroll deduction.

## Retirement

The company contributes 11 percent of base salary to each employee's retirement
fund, paid monthly alongside salary.

## Learning and development

Each employee has a learning budget of 2,000 dollars per fiscal year for courses,
conferences, and books. Unused budget does not carry over. Conference travel
follows the travel policy (see 04-expenses-and-travel.md).

## Wellbeing

All staff have access to a confidential employee assistance program. The company
also offers a wellbeing allowance of 500 dollars per year towards fitness or
wellbeing expenses.

## Referral bonus

Employees who refer a candidate that is hired and passes probation receive a
referral bonus of 3,000 dollars, paid in the month after the referred employee's
probation ends.

## Equity

Senior roles (L4 and above) may be granted equity at hire and at promotion, on a
four-year vesting schedule with a one-year cliff.
