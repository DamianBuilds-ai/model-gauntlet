# Procurement

## Scope

This policy covers the purchase of goods, software, and services on behalf of
Globex.

## Thresholds and approval

Procurement approval mirrors the expense thresholds (see 04-expenses-and-travel.md)
for amount, with these additions for recurring commitments:

- Any new recurring software subscription requires IT review regardless of amount,
  to check security and data-handling fit.
- Contracts with a total value above 25,000 dollars require legal review.
- Contracts above 100,000 dollars require executive sign-off.

## Vendor security review

Any vendor that will process Restricted or Confidential data (see
07-data-handling.md) must pass a security review before a contract is signed. The
review covers the vendor's encryption, access controls, and breach-notification
commitments.

## Purchase orders

Purchases above 2,000 dollars are raised as a purchase order in the finance tool
before the commitment is made. The finance team reconciles purchase orders to
invoices monthly.

## Renewals

Recurring contracts are reviewed 60 days before renewal. The owning team confirms
the contract is still needed and right-sized.
