# Compliance Memo - Globex Insight Data Retention

From: Compliance.
Re: customer-facing data retention for the dashboard.

## Retention requirement (mandated)

- Customer activity and audit events surfaced in the dashboard must be retained and
  displayed for NO MORE than 90 DAYS. This is the mandated retention limit for this
  data class under our current obligations.

- Displaying activity older than 90 days to customers exceeds the permitted retention
  window and is a compliance violation.

## Conflict flag

- The activity-panel PRD states the dashboard shows 12 MONTHS of activity. That
  exceeds the 90-day mandated limit. This MUST be reconciled before launch - the
  product figure cannot stand as written. (Logged as RISK-2 in the risk register.)

## Other notes

- Billing/invoice history has a separate, longer retention basis (contractual) and is
  not governed by this 90-day activity limit. Do not conflate the two.
