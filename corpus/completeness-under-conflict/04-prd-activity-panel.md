# PRD - Activity Panel (Globex Insight)

Owner: Dana (product). Dana is the product owner for the Globex Insight dashboard as a
whole.

## Purpose

The activity panel shows a customer's recent activity feed: logins, key actions, and
audit events over time.

## Data retention shown

- The activity feed shows the trailing 12 MONTHS of activity. We retain and display 12
  months of activity events for the customer.

  (Note: confirm this against the compliance memo's retention requirement.)

## Data sources

- Activity events from the data pipeline.

## Dependencies

- Depends on the data pipeline.
- Depends on dashboard authentication.

## Ownership note

- As the dashboard product owner, Dana signs off panel scope and the dashboard's
  delivery overall.
