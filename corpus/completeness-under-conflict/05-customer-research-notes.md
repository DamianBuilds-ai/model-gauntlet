# Customer Research Notes - Globex Insight

Author: product research.

## Who actually uses the product

- Of the registered base, about 1,200 accounts are MONTHLY ACTIVE (logged in and took
  an action in the trailing 30 days).
- (This is the active figure. The usage-panel PRD quotes ~4,000 REGISTERED accounts -
  a different metric. Active and registered are not the same number and are not in
  conflict.)

## What customers asked for

- A single place to see usage and billing together (today they email support).
- Recent-activity visibility for audit/compliance reasons on their side.

## Friction observed

- Customers found the legacy export flow confusing.
- Several large accounts wanted SSO for their teams (carried into the auth discussion).

## Notes

- Research did not cover the report-builder (out of scope this phase).
