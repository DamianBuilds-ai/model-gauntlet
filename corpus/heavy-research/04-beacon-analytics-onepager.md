Synthetic corpus doc 4 of 16. Vendor one-pager for the managed analytics platform

## Beacon Analytics

A managed analytics platform that bundles the warehouse AND a built-in business
intelligence (dashboards) layer, so you do not buy a separate BI tool.

## Highlights

- Managed, like Lumen - low ops burden.
- Built-in dashboards, so faster time-to-first-dashboard for non-technical users.
- Flat per-seat-plus-base pricing rather than pure consumption (predictable bill).

## Tradeoffs the page is quiet about

- The built-in BI layer is convenient but less flexible than a dedicated BI tool;
  power users sometimes hit its limits (the analyst note, doc 11, expands on this).
- Query latency on large interactive dashboards is its known weak spot - the
  independent benchmark (doc 06) measures this and it is NOT competitive with the
  other two at Riverbend's projected scale.
- EU data residency: see the compliance memo (doc 09) for an important caveat
  specific to Beacon.

## Suited for

Teams that value an all-in-one managed bundle and predictable pricing and whose
interactive-dashboard latency needs are moderate rather than demanding.
