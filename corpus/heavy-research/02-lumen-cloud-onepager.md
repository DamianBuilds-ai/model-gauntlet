# Vendor One-Pager - Lumen Cloud

Synthetic corpus doc 2 of 16. Vendor-supplied marketing one-pager (so read it as
vendor-favourable - it will emphasise strengths and underplay cost).

## Lumen Cloud

A fully managed cloud data warehouse. No servers to run, no patching, auto-scaling
storage and compute.

## Highlights (vendor framing)

- Zero operational burden. Lumen handles upgrades, scaling, backups.
- Separation of storage and compute - scale them independently.
- Strong ecosystem of connectors.
- EU region available (Frankfurt) for data residency.

## Pricing model

Consumption-based: you pay per compute-second and per terabyte stored. The one-pager
calls this "pay only for what you use" and does NOT give a three-year projection. The
real cost picture for Riverbend's growth is in the cost analysis (doc 07), which tells
a more complicated story than this page implies.

## Compliance

The page states Lumen is certified for the common frameworks and offers an EU region.
The deeper compliance detail (and one caveat) is in the compliance memo (doc 09).

## Suited for

Teams that want managed infrastructure and are willing to pay a premium to avoid ops
work. Riverbend's small data team is exactly this profile - but verify the cost
trajectory before concluding.
