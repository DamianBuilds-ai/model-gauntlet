# Vendor Intake Notes - Q2 Supplier Onboarding (raw)

Synthetic corpus for the structured-extraction eval. This is a messy, human-written
intake log dictated by a procurement coordinator across several calls and emails. It
describes EIGHT vendor records. The prose is deliberately inconsistent: some fields
are stated plainly, some are buried mid-sentence, some are implied but never stated,
and some are genuinely absent. There are also a couple of soft traps - a number that
looks like one field but is actually another, and a value mentioned only to be
retracted. All vendors, people, and figures are fictional.

---

Okay, dumping my notes from this round of supplier onboarding before I forget half of
it. Eight of them this quarter, more than usual.

First one, Brightwater Logistics. They're a freight broker out of Hamilton, contact is
Dana Okafor who's their head of accounts - she's the one I've been emailing. Net 30
terms, they were pretty firm on that. Annual spend we're projecting around 240k. They
gave me an ABN, it's 51 824 753 556. Category is logistics, obviously. They're already
onboarded as of the 3rd of April. Oh and their preferred currency is AUD, though for a
freight broker that's a given.

Next, Per Diem Catering. Small outfit, does our Sydney office lunches. The owner Marco
- I never got his surname, he just signs emails "Marco" - quoted us net 14, which is
tighter than I'd like but it's a small spend so whatever. We're looking at maybe 38k a
year, food category. No ABN on file yet, he said he'd send it through and never did, so
that's still outstanding. Status is pending, waiting on that ABN before we activate.

Third, Northbridge Cloud. This is the big one. IT/software category, they host a chunk
of our infra. Contact there is Priya Sundaram, she's the enterprise account lead. They
wanted net 45 which legal pushed back on but eventually we agreed to it. Spend is
significant - I was told to budget 1.1 million for the year. They're a US company so
they invoice in USD. ABN doesn't apply, they gave me their EIN instead which I don't
think we track in this system, so leave the ABN blank. Onboarded 12 February. Active.

Fourth one is a weird one. Company is listed as "TBD Consulting" because honestly we
haven't finalised the entity - the engagement is with a guy named Tom Reilly who's
setting up a new advisory shop and the legal name isn't registered yet. Consulting

he's local, Melbourne based.

Fifth, Greenfield Maintenance. Facilities/maintenance for the Brisbane site. Contact

that was 30 days lead time on parts, NOT payment terms - their actual terms are net 60,

Spend about 85k. ABN 88 102 938 217. Category maintenance. Active since 20 March. AUD.

Sixth, Atlas Stationery. Office supplies, tiny. I genuinely don't remember who the
contact was, some sales rep, never wrote it down. Net 30. Spend is trivial, like 6k a
year. They did give an ABN: 24 600 411 905. Category office supplies. They've been
active for ages, predates this round really, but I'm logging them anyway - active.
Currency AUD.

Seventh. Cardinal Translation Services. Localisation vendor for the product team.
Contact is Yuki Tanaka, title localisation manager. Net 30. They quoted in EUR
initially at around 70k equivalent but - and this matters - we asked them to bill in
AUD instead and they agreed, so the currency on file is AUD, ignore the EUR mention,
that was just their opening quote. ABN 33 555 010 200. Category translation. Status
pending, contract's in review with legal as of writing.

Eighth and last, Coastal Print Co. They print our marketing collateral. I dealt with
two people - the initial contact was someone in sales whose name escapes me, but the
actual relationship owner is Elena Voss, ops manager, so put her down. Net 21, oddly
specific but that's what they run. Spend roughly 52k. Printing category. ABN 19 778
634 122. Active, onboarded 28 February. AUD.

That's the eight. If any of the pending ones convert I'll update. Brightwater and
